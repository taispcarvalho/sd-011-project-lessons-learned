The Two Most Amazing Weeks Ever

I can't actually believe it's been only two weeks since I've started Trybe's Web Development Course.

The learning experience here is like nothing I've ever experienced! From day one, when we received a warm welcome from the amazing team that has been supporting us since, we've been learning something new everyday through practice and experimentation.

From Linux to Git, HTML and CSS. We've been also learning a lot about ourselves and how to better work with others!

Fun Fact

With two weeks of course, I already had to exercise my public speaking skills by giving a presentation about a group project in front of about 170 people! (Virtually, of course.)
